# Notes and Exercisises from CTF Course

This repository consolidates my notes and practical exercises completed during the **CTF Course organized by the cybersecurity club Seek & Hack at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (URJC)**.  
The course focused on developing foundational and intermediate offensive security skills through hands-on challenges, technical sessions, and guided laboratory work.

Unlike standard academic summaries, this repository contains:

- **My own expanded notes**, written from the material taught in the course sessions.
- **Practical exercises (writeups)** developed by me throughout the learning process.
- **Technical analyses**, methodology, and realistic applications of offensive techniques.

The goal is to provide a clean, professional, and practice-oriented resource that reflects my progression in cybersecurity.
